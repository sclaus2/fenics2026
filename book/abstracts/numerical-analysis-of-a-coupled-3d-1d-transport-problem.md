**Submission type:** Presentation

**Presenter:** Uzochi Gideon (Rice University)

A finite element solution coupled with an interior penalty discontinuous Galerkin
solution are defined for the approximation of the coupled 3D-1D solute transport
problem. Under sufficient regularity for the weak solutions, optimal error bounds
are derived for the 3D concentration and 1D concentration, that are optimal
with respect to the time step size and the mesh sizes. Numerical results verify
the theoretical results.
